Integrisoft Solutions

We are focusing mainly on product development & support, custom software and outsourcing services.Given our strong commitment to continuous improvement, we have evolved steadily both in terms of technology and methodology. On the technology side, Integrisoft is proud to have delivered complex solutions using the most advanced technologies, our current focus being on web and mobile applications. As a consequence of the strong direction of using best practices in software development and management, we have been ISO 9001 - 2001 certified since 2008 by TUV Thuringen, ISO 27001

Our dedication to excellence and customer satisfaction promoted our products and services as market leaders in the segments we are addressing (ERP solutions for SME, integrated solutions for local government). We are currently the number one provider of local tax software solutions for local government in Romania. Since this is a highly demanding domain, having high performance requirements and allowing very few errors, we consider we have passed a difficult market test, so we are ready to take on new challenges.

One of our most important strategic directions is investing in people. Corroborated with the sustained success of the last several years, our team has doubled over the last 3 years, despite the harsh economic conditions. Our staff brings to the table a tremendous amount of knowledge and expertise, as well as international and interdisciplinary experience.
