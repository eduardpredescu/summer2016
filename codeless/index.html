Who are we

Codeless Technology is a software integrator with its very unique vision to the software automation process. We are convinced that customers should not be confronted with the option to either choose the high financial risks that come with any customized software development project or the limited options that come with any standard software product. Codeless can offer the best of breed solution in the one and only Codeless® platform. In 1988 Codeless started to develop the Codeless development platform, able to delivery high-end software solutions in different Dutch markets. Our software professionals have gained the unique experience over the last 30 years to efficiently translate any legacy system into the state of the art IT solution. We are a Microsoft Gold partner and use latest Microsoft technologies to implement all our successful solutions. In Codeless we are a highly motivated group of young professionals, open minded with and creative mindset, always taking the initiative to get the best out of ourselves and the solutions we provide. Our team with over 40 industry professionals, is providing software-solutions that are user-friendly, efficient and according to the Mobile & Cloud first principle.
