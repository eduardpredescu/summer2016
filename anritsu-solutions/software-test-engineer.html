Our mission

ANRITSU Solutions Romania is a subsidiary of Anritsu Corporation based in Atsugi, Japan. Recognized as a Tier One provider of communications solutions for 120 years, Anritsu has become a global leader in the telecommunications, optical and wireless industries by providing diverse product offerings ranging from test and measurement solutions to high-speed devices and components for use in R&D, production, and maintenance.

Our team in Bucharest office delivers development and quality assurance services for MasterClaw - the flagship product of Anritsu’s OSS portfolio. Anritsu’s probe-based non-intrusive monitoring system is designed to provide full e2e monitoring of converged networks, being the leading service and network monitoring system for GSM, GPRS, UMTS, SS7 and VoIP networks.
